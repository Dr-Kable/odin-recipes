Spicy Sausage, Egg, & Cheese Scramble

A bold and flavorful scramble packed with savory sausage, melted cheddar, and spicy jalapenos -- perfect for a hearty breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 small jalapeno
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the Eggs:
    • In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Cook the Sausage & Jalapenos:
    • Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
    • Add crumbled sausage and diced jalapenos, cooking for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Scramble the Eggs:
    • Reduce heat to medium-low and pour the egg mixture into the pan.
    • Gently stir with a spatula, allowing eggs to cook slowly while incorporating the sausage and jalapenos.
  4. Add Cheese:
    • When eggs are nearly set, sprinkle in the shredded cheddar.
    • Continue stirring until the cheese melts and the eggs reach your desired doneness.
  5. Serve & Enjoy!
    • Slide onto a plate and enjoy!
