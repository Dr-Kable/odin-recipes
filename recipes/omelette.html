Bacon & Cheddar Omelette

Fluffy Bacon & Cheddar Omelette

A rich, cheesy omelette with crispy bacon --perfect for a hearty breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • 2 strips of cripsy bacon, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the Eggs:
    • In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Cook the Bacon:
    • Cook bacon in a pan over medium heat until crispy, then chop into small pieces.
  3. Make the Omelette:
    • Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat.
    • Pour in the egg mixture and swirl to coat the pan evenly.
    • Let cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.
  4. Add Fillings:
    • Sprinkle bacon and shredded cheddar on one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold & Finish
    • Carefully fold the omelette in half using a sptatula.
    • Cook for another 30 seconds until the cheese melts.
  6. Serve & Enjoy!
    • Slide onto a plate and enjoy!
